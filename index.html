Transforming perspectives through play

About

Tiefblick is a German word that translates to 'deep view', 'deep gaze', or 'deep perception'.
At Tiefblick Studio, we believe in the power of looking beyond the surface and exploring the depth of ourselves to reveal hidden parts of us and bring them to the surface for personal growth and collective empowerment.

Our Vision

We envision a world in which the next generations are empowered to make healthy choices, not only for themselves, but for the collective

Transforming our eco systems, be it socially, environmentally, economically - starts by seeing the interconnectedness of all things.

Building the connection with oneself and finding our own mana, our unique gifts and souls calling is the first step to create lasting change.

Bringing people together to co-create and design the systems of tomorrow, amplifies the evolution and supports our communities needs.

Our Mission

Our social impact games, such as 'Archetypes: The Rite of Passage', are designed to help players explore deeper aspects of their psyche and learn about their strengths and shortcomings in a fun and engaging way.

The stories we tell are addressing real world issues, like mental health, systematic oppression, and social stigma to give a few examples.

We thrive to inspire players to embrace their unique gifts and to utilize the power of community to co-create the future.
